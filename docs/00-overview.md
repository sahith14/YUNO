# YUNO — Product Overview

## One-line pitch
**YUNO is anonymous random video chat for the post-Omegle era — instant, mobile-first, beautifully designed, and obsessive about safety.**

## The opening
In November 2023, Omegle shut down after 14 years. At its peak it served ~70M monthly users with a near-zero engineering team. It died not because demand vanished — demand exploded — but because:

1. The product never evolved past 2009
2. The mobile experience was an afterthought
3. Moderation was outsourced to volunteers and broke under scale
4. The brand became toxic by association with the worst minority of users
5. There was never a sustainable business model

The need it served — *instantaneous, anonymous human contact* — has not been replaced. Discord, Roblox, TikTok Live, Twitch, FaceTime, and a hundred AI-companion apps each address adjacent itches. None of them give you the *random stranger* primitive. That gap is the entire market.

## The product, in one paragraph
You open YUNO. In under three seconds you are face-to-face with a stranger somewhere in the world. You can talk, text, or stay on voice only. If it's not clicking, you tap **Next**. If it is, you keep going — and (if you both agree) you can save the connection. There are no profiles, no bios, no follower counts, no streaks, no notifications begging you to come back. There is just the moment, the stranger, and the choice to stay or go.

## What YUNO is NOT
| Not | Why |
|---|---|
| AI companions / "always-on friends" | The product is humans, not synthesis. Adding AI dilutes the entire premise. |
| Dating app | Dating apps optimize for selection. We optimize for serendipity. Romance can emerge — selection cannot be the funnel. |
| Livestreaming | Streaming is one-to-many. We are strictly one-to-one. |
| Social network | No graph, no posts, no feed. The unit of value is the conversation, not the timeline. |
| Anonymous shitposting | Anonymity is the *delivery mechanism*, not the brand. The brand is *human warmth*. |

## Core principles

1. **Time-to-stranger < 3 seconds.** This is the only metric that defines the product. Everything else serves it.
2. **No fake humans.** No bots, no AI personas, no pre-recorded loops. Verification happens, but the experience is unmistakably real.
3. **Mobile-first, always.** 70%+ of usage will be vertical, single-handed, on the bus.
4. **Skip is sacred.** A skip must feel weightless — no penalties, no friction, no "are you sure?".
5. **Safety is design, not a layer.** Moderation, reporting, and reputation are first-class UX, not afterthoughts.
6. **The product is the brand.** Cinematic dark mode, micro-animations, tactile sounds — every surface signals craft.
7. **Free is sustainable.** Premium funds free. Verified Female covers infra. We never gate the core primitive.

## Target users (in priority order)

| Segment | % of usage | Why they come |
|---|---|---|
| 18–24 night-owls | 45% | Boredom, loneliness, novelty, dopamine |
| 25–34 expats / shift-workers | 20% | Casual connection without commitment |
| Language learners | 15% | Free practice with native speakers |
| Travelers / curious / social anthropologists | 10% | Glimpse other lives |
| Verified-mode users (any gender) | 10% | Want filtered, safer interactions |

## What success looks like (90 days post-launch)

- 100k installs
- 25k DAU
- 6 minutes median session length
- 4 conversations per session
- 1.5% free → premium conversion
- < 1% session-with-violation rate
- < 5 min median report-to-action time
